Greek Culture & Idioms

Understanding Greek culture and idioms is essential for mastering the language and connecting with Greek speakers.

Greek Cultural Etiquette

  • Philoxenia: The famous Greek hospitality - Greeks are known for their warm welcome to strangers
  • Family Values: Family is central to Greek life, with strong intergenerational bonds
  • Gesture Communication: Greeks often use hand gestures to emphasize speech
  • Mealtime: Lunch is typically the main meal, eaten around 2-3 PM
  • Name Days: Celebrating one's name day (day of the saint they're named after) is often more important than birthdays

Greek Superstitions

  • Matiasma (Evil Eye): The belief that someone can harm you with an envious glance
